Option Stochastic Volatility Model

Traditional Black-Scholes model assumes the underlying asset has a constant volatility, which proves unrealistic in practice. To address this problem, stochastic volatility model (another SDE is used to describe the dynamics of the volatility) such as Heston, variance gamma (VGSA) was introduced. This projects collects real word data to calibrate these models and produces a local volatility surface implied by the models
